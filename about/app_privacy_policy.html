1. 概述

gasgx（以下简称"我们"）开发并运营本应用程序。我们深知个人信息对您的重要性，并承诺遵守适用的法律法规（包括GDPR、CCPA等）以保护您的隐私。

本隐私政策解释了当您使用我们的服务（特别是涉及Google API服务）时，我们要如何收集、使用、披露和保护您的信息。

2. 我们收集的信息

我们需要收集特定信息以提供服务，主要包括：

  • 账户信息：您的姓名、电子邮件地址、个人资料图片（通过登录获取）。
  • Google用户数据：经您明确授权，我们通过Google API访问的数据（详情见下文）。
  • 使用数据：日志文件、设备IP地址、浏览器类型、操作行为等。

3. Google用户数据的使用与披露（重要）

如果我们的应用请求访问您的Google数据，我们将严格遵守以下原则：

3.1 访问的数据范围

我们仅请求运行应用核心功能所需的最低权限。具体请求的权限范围包括：

  • 基本账户信息 (userinfo.email, userinfo.profile)： 用于验证您的身份并在应用中显示您的个人资料。
  • Google Drive 文件 (drive.readonly)： 仅访问您在应用内明确选择导入或处理的文件/文档。
  • Gmail 邮件内容 (gmail.readonly)： 仅用于读取您指定的邮件内容以进行分析或摘要。
  • 代表您发送邮件 (gmail.send)： 仅在您明确点击发送按钮并确认内容后，代表您发送回复或草稿。

3.2 数据使用目的

我们访问Google用户数据仅用于以下目的：

  • 身份验证：允许您安全登录并管理您的账户。
  • 文档与邮件处理：读取文档或邮件内容，为您提供智能摘要、格式转换、数据提取或自动回复建议服务。
  • 服务执行：根据您的指令处理文件或发送邮件。

我们不会将您的Google用户数据用于广告目的，也不会将其出售给第三方。我们不会人工阅读您的邮件或文件内容，所有处理均由自动化程序完成。

3.3 遵守"有限使用"政策 (Limited Use Policy)

gasgx对从Google API收到的信息的使用和转移到任何其他应用程序，将遵守Google API服务用户数据政策，包括"有限使用"要求。

4. 数据共享与第三方服务

除非获得您的同意或法律要求，否则我们不会与第三方共享您的个人信息。以下情况除外：

  • 服务提供商：我们可能使用第三方服务（如云托管服务商AWS/Google Cloud）来协助运营，这些方只能根据我们的指示处理数据。
  • AI模型处理：为了提供智能分析、摘要或写作建议服务，您的非个人身份数据（如文档文本段落）可能会发送至第三方AI模型提供商（如 OpenAI/Claude）进行处理。我们已与提供商签署严格协议，明确禁止其利用您的数据来训练或改进其通用AI模型。您的数据仅用于单次处理，处理完成后不作留存。

5. 数据存储与保留

您的数据存储在加密的安全服务器上。我们仅在提供服务所需的期间内保留您的个人信息。对于通过API临时获取的文件或邮件内容，我们在处理完成后不会在服务器上永久保留原始副本。

当您注销账户或撤销授权时，我们将根据相关法律删除或匿名化处理您的数据。

6. 您的权利

您拥有以下权利：

  • 访问、更正或删除您的个人信息。
  • 通过Google安全设置页面(https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)随时撤销对我们应用的授权。

1. Overview

gasgx (hereinafter referred to as "we") develops and operates this application. We understand the importance of personal information and are committed to complying with applicable laws and regulations (including GDPR, CCPA, etc.) to protect your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services, especially those involving Google API Services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect specific information necessary to provide our services, including:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture (obtained via login).
  • Google User Data: Data accessed via Google APIs with your explicit authorization (details below).
  • Usage Data: Log files, IP addresses, browser type, and interaction data.

3. Use and Disclosure of Google User Data (Important)

If our application requests access to your Google data, we strictly adhere to the following principles:

3.1 Scope of Data Access

We request only the minimum permissions necessary to run the application's core functions. Specific requested scopes include:

  • Basic Account Info (userinfo.email, userinfo.profile): Used to authenticate your identity and display your profile within the app.
  • Google Drive Files (drive.readonly): Accesses only the specific files or documents you explicitly select to import or process.
  • Gmail Content (gmail.readonly): Used to read content of emails you specify for analysis or summarization.
  • Send Emails on your behalf (gmail.send): Executed only when you explicitly click a send button and confirm the content (e.g., sending replies or drafts).

3.2 Purpose of Data Use

We access Google user data solely for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To allow you to securely log in and manage your account.
  • Document & Email Processing: To read content to provide you with intelligent summaries, format conversion, data extraction, or automated reply suggestions.
  • Service Execution: To process files or send emails based on your direct instructions.

We do not use your Google user data for advertising purposes, nor do we sell it to third parties. We do not have humans read your emails or files; all processing is done by automated procedures.

3.3 Limited Use Policy Compliance

gasgx's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless we have your consent or are required by law, except for:

  • Service Providers: We may use third-party services (e.g., Cloud providers like AWS/Google Cloud) to assist in operations. These parties process data only according to our instructions.
  • AI Model Processing: To provide intelligent analysis, summarization, or writing assistance, your non-personally identifiable data (such as document text segments) may be processed by third-party AI model providers (e.g., OpenAI/Claude). We have strict agreements prohibiting them from using your data to train or improve their general AI models. Your data is used for single-instance processing and is not retained thereafter.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored on encrypted, secure servers. We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services. For files or email content accessed temporarily via API, we do not permanently retain original copies on our servers after processing is complete.

When you delete your account or revoke authorization, we will delete or anonymize your data in accordance with applicable laws.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Revoke authorization for our app via the Google Security Settings page (https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).
